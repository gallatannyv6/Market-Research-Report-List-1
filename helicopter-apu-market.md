<p><ul><li>Electric APU</li><li>Pneumatic APU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The helicopter Auxiliary Power Units (APUs) market is primarily divided into two types: electric APUs and pneumatic APUs. Electric APUs utilize battery-powered systems to provide electrical energy for avionics and other onboard systems, promoting efficiency and reducing dependence on main engines during ground operations. In contrast, pneumatic APUs generate compressed air, which can start engines or supply climate control. Both types enhance operational flexibility, support maintenance needs, and improve overall energy management in helicopters.</p></p>
