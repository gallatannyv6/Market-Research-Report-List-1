<p><p>The lithium battery manufacturing machinery market encompasses three key types: pretreatment, cell assembly, and post-processing. Pretreatment involves preparing raw materials and components, ensuring they meet specifications for optimal performance. Cell assembly refers to the actual construction of battery cells, integrating electrodes, separators, and electrolytes through precise machinery. Post-processing encompasses finishing steps such as formation, aging, and testing, which enhance battery performance and safety. Together, these categories contribute to the efficient production of high-quality lithium batteries.</p></p>
