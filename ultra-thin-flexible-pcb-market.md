<p><p>The ultra-thin flexible PCB market is categorized into two main types: single layer and multi-layer. Single-layer PCBs consist of one conductive layer that is simple and cost-effective, ideal for basic applications. In contrast, multi-layer PCBs feature multiple conductive layers, allowing for more complex designs and enhanced functionality, making them suitable for advanced electronics in sectors like automotive and medical devices. Both types offer advantages in terms of flexibility, weight, and space-saving, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
