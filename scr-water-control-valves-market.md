<p><p>The SCR Water Control Valves market comprises primarily two types: 2-way and 3-way valves. 2-way valves function to control the flow of water in a single direction, making them ideal for on-off applications. In contrast, 3-way valves regulate flow between two outlets, allowing for mixing or diverting functions, enhancing versatility in system configurations. Both types are essential in various industries, including HVAC, irrigation, and process control, providing efficient management of water distribution and usage in different applications.</p></p>
