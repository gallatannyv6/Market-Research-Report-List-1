<p><strong>The TMR Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Output</li><li>Analog Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TMR (Tunnel Magnetoresistance) sensors are utilized for precise magnetic field detection and are categorized into two main market types: digital output and analog output. Digital output TMR sensors convert magnetic signals into discrete digital signals, providing clear on/off states, making them suitable for applications like position sensing and contactless switches. In contrast, analog output TMR sensors deliver continuous voltage signals proportional to the magnetic field strength, ideal for applications requiring detailed measurements, such as automotive and industrial automation systems.</p></p>
