<p><p>The Metal Laboratory Animal Housing Cage market consists of two primary types: disposable cages and reusable cages. Disposable cages are designed for single-use, ensuring hygienic conditions and reducing the risk of cross-contamination between experiments. These are often made from lightweight materials for convenient disposal. In contrast, reusable cages are constructed for durability and can withstand multiple sterilization cycles. They are cost-effective over time, suitable for long-term studies, and often feature customizable designs for various species and research needs.</p></p>
