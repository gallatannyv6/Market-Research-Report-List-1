<p><p>The citric acid powder market consists of two primary types: citric acid anhydrous and citric acid monohydrate. Citric acid anhydrous is a dehydrated form, typically used in food preservation, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics due to its concentrated properties and stability. On the other hand, citric acid monohydrate contains water molecules, making it suitable for applications where moisture is needed, such as in beverages and confectionery. Both types are essential for various industries, contributing to flavor enhancement and acidity regulation.</p></p>
