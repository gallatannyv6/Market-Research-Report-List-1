<p><ul><li>Orthotics</li><li>Prosthetics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The animal ortho-prosthetics market is divided into two primary types: orthotics and prosthetics. Orthotics involve supportive devices designed to enhance the function of existing limbs, aiding animals with joint, muscle, or skeletal issues. In contrast, prosthetics refer to artificial limbs that replace missing or severely damaged limbs, enabling mobility and improving the quality of life for animals. Both segments are driven by advancements in materials and technology, fostering greater accessibility and customization for various animal species.</p></p>
