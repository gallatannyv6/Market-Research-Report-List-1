<p><p>Gas Insulated Transmission Lines (GITLs) market consists of various installation types catering to different environmental and spatial requirements. **Aboveground Installation** utilizes towers to support the lines, suitable for urban areas with limited space. **Tunnel Installation** involves placing lines within underground tunnels, minimizing surface disruption. **Vertical Installation** is characterized by lines equipped vertically, often saving ground space. **Direct Burial** entails installing GITLs directly underground, providing protection from environmental factors and optimizing land use, particularly in densely populated regions.</p></p>
