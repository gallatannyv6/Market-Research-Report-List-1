<p><p>The decorative glass market is segmented into three main types: Low-E glass, special glass, and other types. Low-E (low emissivity) glass is designed to reduce heat transfer, enhancing energy efficiency while maintaining transparency. Special glass includes products like tempered, laminated, and patterned glass, offering unique aesthetics and safety features. The "other" category encompasses various decorative options, such as stained, frosted, or etched glass. Each type caters to distinct architectural and design needs, combining functionality with visual appeal.</p></p>
