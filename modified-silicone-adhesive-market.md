<p><ul><li>Neutral Cure</li><li>Moisture Cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modified silicone adhesive market can be categorized into two primary types: neutral cure and moisture cure. Neutral cure adhesives do not release acetic acid during curing, making them suitable for sensitive materials like metals and plastics, as they minimize corrosion and odor. In contrast, moisture cure adhesives rely on ambient moisture for curing, forming strong bonds in various conditions. These types are widely used in construction, automotive, and electronics due to their flexibility, durability, and resistance to environmental factors.</p></p>
