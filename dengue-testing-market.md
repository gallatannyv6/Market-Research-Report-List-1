<p><strong>The Dengue Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ELISA-Based Tests</li><li>RT-PCR Based Tests</li><li>Dengue IgG/IgM Rapid Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dengue testing market comprises several key types. ELISA-based tests utilize enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay technology to detect dengue-specific antibodies, providing accurate and reliable results for diagnosis. RT-PCR-based tests employ reverse transcription polymerase chain reaction to identify viral RNA, offering rapid results during early infection stages. Dengue IgG/IgM rapid tests are simple, point-of-care diagnostics that detect immunoglobulin G and M antibodies, enabling quick assessments and facilitating timely disease management, especially in resource-limited settings.</p></p>
