<p><ul><li>XHV Valve</li><li>UHV Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum all-metal valves market is primarily categorized into XHV (Extreme High Vacuum) valves and UHV (Ultra High Vacuum) valves. XHV valves are designed for applications requiring pressures below 10^-9 Torr, offering superior leak rates and thermal stability, making them ideal for cutting-edge research and semiconductor manufacturing. UHV valves, operating in the range of 10^-9 to 10^-7 Torr, are commonly used in industries such as aerospace and materials science, providing reliable containment in high-vacuum environments.</p></p>
