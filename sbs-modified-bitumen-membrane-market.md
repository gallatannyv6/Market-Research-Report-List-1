<p><p>The SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) modified bitumen membrane market features three main types: Base/Interply Membrane, Base/Ply Membrane, and Cap Sheets. Base/Interply membranes are designed for use as the primary layer or reinforcement in multi-layer roofing systems, offering flexibility and durability. Base/Ply membranes serve as the foundational layer, providing strength and stability. Cap sheets are applied as the top layer, enhancing weather resistance and aesthetics. These membranes are vital in commercial and residential roofing for their waterproofing and protective properties.</p></p>
