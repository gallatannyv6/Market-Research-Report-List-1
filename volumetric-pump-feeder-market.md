<p><ul><li>Single Screw</li><li>Twin Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The volumetric pump feeder market comprises two main types: single screw and twin screw. Single screw feeders utilize a single rotating screw to transport materials, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, suitable for various applications. Twin screw feeders feature two intermeshing screws that provide enhanced control over material flow and mixing, making them ideal for precise dosing and handling viscous materials. Both types are essential in industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and plastics, contributing to efficient material processing and production.</p></p>
