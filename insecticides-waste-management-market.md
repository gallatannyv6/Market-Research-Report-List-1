<p><p>Insecticides waste management encompasses various treatment methods to safely dispose of or recycle hazardous residues. Mechanical Biological Treatment (MBT) involves physical processing and biological decomposition to reduce waste volume and toxicity. Incineration employs high-temperature combustion to convert waste into ash, gases, and heat, effectively eliminating harmful substances. Anaerobic Digestion utilizes microorganisms in oxygen-free environments to break down organic materials, producing biogas and digestate, while mitigating odors and reducing landfill reliance. Together, these approaches address environmental and health concerns associated with insecticides.</p></p>
