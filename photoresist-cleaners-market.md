<p><strong>The Photoresist Cleaners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist (PR) Strippers</li><li>Negative Photoresist (PR) Strippers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoresist cleaners market is primarily categorized into positive and negative photoresist strippers. Positive photoresist strippers are designed to remove photoresist layers where the exposed areas become soluble during the development process. Conversely, negative photoresist strippers target resist layers that harden when exposed to light, necessitating different chemical formulations for effective removal. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing and microfabrication, ensuring clean surfaces for subsequent processing steps and enhancing the overall yield and quality of electronic devices.</p></p>
