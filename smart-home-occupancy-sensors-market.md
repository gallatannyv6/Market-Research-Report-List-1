<p><p>The smart home occupancy sensors market includes several types: </p><p>1. **PIR Sensors** detect infrared radiation from human body heat, making them efficient for detecting movement in designated areas.</p><p>2. **Ultrasonic Sensors** use sound waves to detect motion, allowing them to sense occupancy even behind obstacles. </p><p>3. **Dual-technology Occupancy Sensors** combine PIR and ultrasonic technologies for enhanced accuracy, reducing false positives. </p><p>4. **Others** may encompass advanced technologies like microwave and photoelectric sensors, offering diverse applications in smart home automation.</p></p>
