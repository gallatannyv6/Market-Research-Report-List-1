<p><strong>The Neural Control Interface Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Invasive</li><li>Noninvasive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Neural Control Interface (NCI) market is divided into two primary types: invasive and non-invasive. Invasive NCIs involve surgical procedures to place electrodes directly on or within the brain, offering high-resolution control and data acquisition for applications like prosthetics and neurorehabilitation. Conversely, non-invasive NCIs utilize external methods, such as electroencephalography (EEG), to interpret brain signals without surgery, making them safer and more accessible for applications in gaming, mental health monitoring, and cognitive enhancement. Each type serves distinct user needs and safety considerations.</p></p>
