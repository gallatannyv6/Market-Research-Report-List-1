<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Sterilization Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat Sterilization Equipment</li><li>Chemical Sterilization</li><li>Filtration Sterilization</li><li>Ionizing Radiation Sterilization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical sterilization equipment market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Heat Sterilization Equipment** utilizes high temperatures to eliminate microorganisms, often through autoclaving.</p><p>2. **Chemical Sterilization** employs chemical agents, like ethylene oxide, to achieve sterility at low temperatures, suitable for heat-sensitive items.</p><p>3. **Filtration Sterilization** involves passing fluids or gases through filters to remove pathogens, commonly used for antibiotics and vaccines.</p><p>4. **Ionizing Radiation Sterilization** uses gamma rays or electron beams to disrupt DNA and effectively sterilize various products, including disposables and packaging.</p></p>
