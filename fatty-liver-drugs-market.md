<p><p>The fatty liver drugs market is primarily categorized into Alcoholic Liver Disease (ALD) and Non-Alcoholic Fatty Liver Disease (NAFLD). In contrast, NAFLD is characterized by fat accumulation in the liver without alcohol-related causes, often linked to obesity and metabolic syndrome. The treatment for NAFLD focuses on lifestyle changes and medications that target metabolic factors and inflammation.</p></p>
