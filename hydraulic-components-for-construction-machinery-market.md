<p><strong>The Hydraulic Components for Construction Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Power Element</li><li>Actuator</li><li>Control Element</li><li>Auxiliary Components</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic components for construction machinery market comprises four main types: </p><p>1. **Power Element**: These generate hydraulic power, including pumps and motors that convert mechanical energy into hydraulic energy.  </p><p>2. **Actuator**: These devices, such as hydraulic cylinders and motors, convert hydraulic energy into mechanical movement.  </p><p>3. **Control Element**: Components like valves regulate fluid flow and pressure, ensuring precise control over machinery functions.  </p><p>4. **Auxiliary Components**: This category includes filters, hoses, and connectors that support the hydraulic system's efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
