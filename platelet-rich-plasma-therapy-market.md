<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Platelet-rich Plasma Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pure PRP</li><li>Leucocyte-rich PRP</li><li>Leukocyte-rich Fibrin (L-PRF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Platelet-rich Plasma (PRP) therapy encompasses various types tailored for specific therapeutic applications. Pure PRP contains a high concentration of platelets with minimal leukocytes, ideal for tasks like skin rejuvenation and hair restoration. Leucocyte-rich PRP includes a higher level of white blood cells, enhancing healing and is often used in wound care and joint therapies. Leukocyte-rich Fibrin (L-PRF) combines platelets and leukocytes in a fibrin matrix, promoting tissue regeneration and is frequently employed in oral surgery and regenerative medicine.</p></p>
